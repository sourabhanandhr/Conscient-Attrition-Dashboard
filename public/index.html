Key findings for leadership action
86% of exits are Junior-level employees (36/42) — suggesting early-career retention is the #1 priority. Performance Issues + Work Pressure together drive 60% of all departures. Bangalore (36.7%) and Pune (31.8%) are significantly above the company average of 24.4% and need localised intervention. Female attrition rate (30%) is higher than male (24%) despite a smaller workforce — worth monitoring.
